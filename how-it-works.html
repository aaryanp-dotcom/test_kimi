How MindSpace Works

Getting started with online therapy is simple. Follow these easy steps to begin your journey to better mental health.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free MindSpace account in just a few minutes. We'll ask for some basic information to help us understand your needs better. Your information is always kept confidential and secure.

  • Provide your name and email address
  • Create a secure password
  • Complete your profile (optional but recommended)
Sign Up Now
2

Find Your Therapist

Browse our directory of licensed therapists and filter by specialization, price, and availability. Each therapist profile includes their background, areas of expertise, and approach to therapy.

  • Search by specialization (anxiety, depression, relationships, etc.)
  • Compare fees and experience levels
  • Read therapist bios to find the right match
Browse Therapists
3

Book Your Session

Once you've found a therapist you'd like to work with, booking a session is quick and easy. Choose a date and time that works for you, and provide some information about what you'd like to discuss.

  • Select your preferred date and time
  • Share what you'd like to focus on
  • Complete payment securely
4

Attend Your Session

Join your video session at the scheduled time using the secure meeting link provided by your therapist. All you need is a device with a camera and internet connection.

  • Find a quiet, private space
  • Test your camera and microphone
  • Click the meeting link to join
5

Continue Your Journey

Therapy is a process, and we're here to support you every step of the way. Book follow-up sessions, manage your appointments, and track your progress through your personal dashboard.

  • Schedule follow-up sessions
  • Request reschedules if needed
  • Access session notes from your therapist
