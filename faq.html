Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindSpace and our services.

General Questions

What is MindSpace?

MindSpace is an online therapy platform that connects individuals with licensed mental health professionals. We provide a convenient, affordable, and confidential way to access therapy from the comfort of your home.

How does online therapy work?

Online therapy works similarly to in-person therapy, but sessions are conducted via secure video calls. You can schedule appointments, meet with your therapist, and even receive session notesâ€”all through our platform.

Is online therapy effective?

Yes! Research has shown that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many mental health concerns. It offers the added benefits of convenience, accessibility, and comfort.

For Clients

How do I choose a therapist?

You can browse our directory of therapists and filter by specialization, price, and experience. Read their bios to learn about their approach and background. If you're not sure, you can contact us for guidance.

How much does therapy cost?

Therapy fees vary by therapist, typically ranging from $50 to $200 per session. Each therapist sets their own rate, which is clearly displayed on their profile. There are no hidden fees.

Can I reschedule or cancel a session?

Yes, you can request to reschedule or cancel pending and confirmed appointments through your dashboard. Please give your therapist as much notice as possible.

What if I don't like my therapist?

Finding the right therapist is important. If you feel your current therapist isn't a good fit, you're free to browse and book sessions with other therapists on our platform.

Privacy & Security

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are confidential and protected by the same privacy laws that apply to in-person therapy. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your consent.

How are video sessions secured?

All video sessions use encrypted connections (HTTPS) to ensure your conversations remain private. We recommend using secure, private internet connections for your sessions.

For Therapists

How do I join as a therapist?

Click "Join as Therapist" and complete the application form. We'll review your credentials and approve your profile within 1-2 business days. You'll need to provide proof of licensure.

What are the requirements to join?

You must be a licensed mental health professional with active credentials in your jurisdiction. Requirements include: valid license, professional liability insurance, and at least 2 years of clinical experience.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. You'll receive payouts to your linked bank account on a weekly basis for completed sessions.
